ACCOMMODATION

Great for outdoor enthusiasts or reluctant first time campers seeking the romance of sleeping in the outdoors our campsite comes with amenities like proper washrooms, running water, 3 meals a day and great ambience.
Located in the Native Place Garden under a shade canopy, fringed by clumps of whispering bamboo with a large picnic bench to chill out by as you gaze out at a lovely lake view. We have Tents to accommodate around 20 persons

TENTS

Come experience outdoor living without the accompanied hardships. We offer you tents on a twin and triple sharing basis in the lovely Native Place garden. Wake up to the sound of chirping birds, fall asleep lulled by the buzzing sounds of crickets and cicadas or keep the flap up and stare up at the stars.
The tents are set up with mattresses, sheets and pillows and also have a protective insect meshing so you can enjoy a peaceful nomadic stay.
