ACTIVITIES

A stay at the Native Place guesthouse gives you the essence of village life without, however, the accompanied hardships. Here you can indulge in nature’s delights, while children can learn about sustainable ecological Indian village life that is fast disappearing.
Native Place is a great weekend destination, but it is not just a one-night destination. Although we would be perfectly happy for you if you choose to lounge around and simply ‘do nothing’, which we believe is an art in itself; we have laid out some options of what you could do here over a few days.

Here is list of things you can do on your own at Native Place.

• Native Place Discovery: We have compiled a series of Garden Discovery cards for you to explore the many delights and treasures in the garden. You will find them on a stand by the dining table. We urge you to use them and share your findings with us on our facebook page - Bird Watching - From Grey Hornbills to Thrushes, you may catch lucky glimpses of some exciting birds in the trees around
- Trees Exploration - Find a fascinating collection of indigenous trees, which have many stories to tell.
- Fragrant Trails - From local Wildflowers to exotic Angel’s Trumpet, our garden has a unique range of flower plants
- Amazing Climbers - Get introduced to Jade Vine and other colourful creepers
- Shrubs and Medicinal Plants - Learn about the natural remedies available in our garden plants
- Garden Scavenger Hunt - Pick this card up and go a wild goose chase
• A visit to the Lake: Go for leisure walks by the lake, and don’t miss out on the surreal sunsets
• Hill Walking - The hills are very easy to explore
• Visit to Ancient Buddhist Caves (You can visit this place in your own vehicle, or we can hire a vehicle for you)
• Visit to flying sites (You can visit this place in your own vehicle, or we can hire a vehicle for you)
• Stargazing - Don’t miss out on Meteor Showers, which can be viewed from the Native Place terrace
